Welcome to Minnesota Meditation Center

Minnesota Meditation Center is a Dhammakaya Buddhist temple welcoming all who seek inner peace and mindfulness. Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced practitioner, you'll find a warm community here.

All meditation classes are FREE. No experience necessary. Comfortable seating options available for everyone.
