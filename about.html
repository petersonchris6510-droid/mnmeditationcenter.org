About Us

A Buddhist meditation center serving the Twin Cities community since 2006.

Our Practice

Minnesota Meditation Center is a Buddhist meditation center rooted in the Dhammakaya tradition of Buddhism, which emphasizes meditation as the path to inner peace and spiritual understanding. Our practice focuses on:

  • Meditation for Inner Peace: Learning to find stillness and clarity through guided meditation
  • Mindfulness in Daily Life: Bringing awareness and compassion to everyday experiences
  • Community Support: Growing together in a welcoming, supportive environment
  • Accessible Teachings: Buddhist wisdom presented in ways that anyone can understand and apply

Whether you're new to meditation or have been practicing for years, our tradition welcomes seekers of all backgrounds.

Our Mission

To provide a peaceful, welcoming space where anyone can learn to meditate, find stillness, and connect with a community that genuinely cares about their wellbeing.

Everyone Is Welcome

Our center serves both the broader Twin Cities community and Minnesota's Thai and Lao communities. We offer English-language meditation classes and traditional Thai-language services.
